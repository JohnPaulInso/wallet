Privacy Policy
update Last updated: February 24, 2026

Smart Wallet ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance tracking application developed by John Paul Inso. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

info

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we access the following information:

  • Google Account Profile — Your name, email address, and profile picture for authentication and personalization.
  • Gmail (Read-Only) — If you enable Gmail Sync, we scan your inbox for bank transaction notification emails to automatically log expenses. We only read email subject lines and body content relevant to financial transactions.
verified_user

How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication — We use Firebase Authentication to securely sign you in and identify your account.
  • Transaction Tracking — Gmail data is processed in your browser to extract transaction details (merchant, amount, date). This data is stored in your personal Firebase Firestore database.
  • Personalization — Your name and profile picture are displayed within the app for a personalized experience.
shield

Data Storage & Security

  • Your financial data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, linked to your authenticated Google account.
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Gmail content is processed entirely in your browser — we do not store raw email content on any server.
  • Access tokens are stored locally on your device and are never shared with third parties.
block

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your data is only used within the Smart Wallet app for the purposes described above. The only third-party services we use are Google Firebase (for authentication and data storage) and Google APIs (for Gmail access), both governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

tune

Your Rights & Controls

  • Revoke Access — You can revoke Smart Wallet's access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account Permissions.
  • Delete Data — You can export and delete all your transaction data from within the app using the Account Backup feature.
  • Log Out — Logging out clears your session. Your data remains in Firestore until you choose to delete it.
history

Data Retention

We retain your transaction data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your data, you may do so through the app's export/import functionality or by contacting us directly.
